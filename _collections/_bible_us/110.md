1 Samuel 25 recounts the conflict between David and Nabal, and how Abigail intervenes with wisdom, preventing a tragedy and changing the course of the story. Read the full text {{ "here" | hyperlink: "https://www.bibliaonline.com.br/niv/1sm/25", true }}.

---

## Text Structure

### 1Sm 25:1-8

**David’s request**. David sends messengers to Nabal asking for provisions, reminding him of the protection previously offered, expecting a response of recognition and cooperation.

### 1Sm 25:9-13

**Nabal’s response**. Nabal reacts with contempt and refuses to help, provoking indignation in David, who decides to act impulsively and prepares for revenge.

### 1Sm 25:14-22

The **planned intervention**. Upon hearing what happened, Abigail acts quickly, preparing provisions and going to meet David to prevent serious consequences.

### 1Sm 25:23-31

**Abigail’s wisdom**. Abigail humbles herself and speaks with discernment, recognizing David’s purpose and preventing him from making an impulsive mistake.

### 1Sm 25:32-35

The **change of decision**. David acknowledges the intervention and abandons his plan for revenge, showing openness to correction.

### 1Sm 25:36-38

The **judgment on Nabal**. Nabal suffers the consequences of his attitude, showing that God deals with each situation with justice.

### 1Sm 25:39-44

The **outcome and new paths**. David recognizes God’s action, and Abigail becomes part of his story, marking a new chapter.

## Reflection

1 Samuel 25 shows how impulsive decisions can lead to serious consequences. David was about to act based on emotion.

Abigail’s attitude reveals the power of wisdom and timely intervention. A right action can completely change a situation.

This passage teaches that listening and reconsidering are signs of maturity, helping to avoid mistakes that could compromise the future.
