Genesis 24:52-67 recounts the conclusion of the servant’s mission, Rebekah’s decision, and her meeting with Isaac, highlighting God’s care in every step. Read the full text {{ "here" | hyperlink: "https://www.bibliaonline.com.br/niv/gn/24/52-67", true }}.

---

## Text Structure

### Gen 24:52-54

The **confirmation and gratitude**. The servant worships God for the answer received and offers gifts, recognizing the divine guidance in the mission.

### Gen 24:55-58

Rebekah’s **decision**. Faced with the proposal, Rebekah agrees to leave immediately, demonstrating willingness and trust in the purpose.

### Gen 24:59-61

The **farewell and blessing**. The family says goodbye and declares blessings over Rebekah, marking the beginning of a new stage in her life.

### Gen 24:62-65

The **meeting with Isaac**. Rebekah sees Isaac and prepares to meet him, while he was meditating in the field.

### Gen 24:66-67

The **union and comfort**. Isaac receives Rebekah as his wife and finds comfort after his mother’s death.

## Reflection

Genesis 24:52-67 shows that when God leads a process, He also completes every step. What began with prayer ends with fulfillment and joy.

Rebekah’s decision reveals faith in action. Even in the face of the unknown, she chooses to go, trusting in God’s direction for her life.

As presented in **Patriarchs and Prophets**, chapter 15, this moment shows that God not only guides paths but also establishes relationships according to His purpose. When there is willingness to obey, He leads to fulfillment.
